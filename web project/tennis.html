Tour de France to start in Italy for first time in 2024

The Tour de France will start in Italy for the first time ever in 2024. It will begin in Florence in a year marking the centenary of Ottavio Bottecchia's Tour de France win, which was the first by an Italian, in 1924. The first three stages will take place in Italy, while the race will finish in Nice in another first. The conclusion will be outside of the Paris region for the first time in the race's history because the Olympics start days later in the French capital.
